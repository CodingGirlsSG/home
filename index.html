Who Are We?

A Singapore registered non-profit organization
, an introductory platform for women from all walks of life to connect and learn more about the tech field.
- We are exclusively the only local initiative that strives to eliminate and lower barriers to entry for women who have little to almost no knowledge in technology but are passionate about cultivating or honing these skills.
- We are a welcoming community for women who shy away from the tech industry because the common issue of gender bias in the field.

Our Programs

What do we do?

INSPIRATION

Female representation in Technology matters greatly. Through our regular tech talks that feature successful women the industry, we hope to inspire a new generation of women to pursue careers in this industry.

EDUCATION

We believe that technical skills such as computer programming are empowering. Through our introductory courses, we make technology education options more accessible for women to pick up the skills they need to thrive in an increasingly digital world.

CONNECTION

We provide the platform for women from all walks of life to connect with other like-minded women as they embark on their journey in the tech industry.
