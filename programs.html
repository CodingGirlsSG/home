Our Programs

Free Coding Workshops

We organize free entry-level coding workshop for women who are interested in programming. Our workshop including  topics like Web Development , Mob App development or Data Analytics skills like Python,   R , SQL etc

Bootcamp

Our Bootcamp are designed to help students to enhance their skillsets in fields such as web development and data analytics. Our boot camps are based on real-life projects which means that at the end of the courses, students are required to complete a project to demonstrate their learning outcome and problem solving abilities.

Tech Talks

Engaging, relevant content developed and delivered by industry leading experts. These onstage demos offer a mind-blowing peek at where technology is taking us.

With Her

We invited female experts, entrepreneurs who are leaders in their domain as members’ mentors and share their experience with the crowd.

CodingGirls Day

CodingGirls Day brings female tech leaders, founders and enthusiasts in Southeast Asia together for a day of sharing, networking and networking.

We aim to create an opportunity for women from all walks of life to get in touch with the tech community, connect them with leaders and show them the path to excel in tech career.
